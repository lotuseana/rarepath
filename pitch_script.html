RarePath

1-Minute Pitch · HackHayward 2026
The Problem
300 million people worldwide have a rare disease. The average patient waits 5 to 7 years and sees 8 different doctors before getting a correct diagnosis. And 95% of these diseases have no approved treatment — so early diagnosis is everything.
The Solution
RarePath fixes this. A clinician enters symptoms — typed or spoken into an Omi wearable — and in under 15 seconds, they get a ranked differential diagnosis across 4,335 rare diseases, with confirmatory tests, cost estimates optimized for low-resource settings, live research citations pulled from current literature, and a voice briefing in any of 10 languages for the patient's family.
Federated Learning
Under the hood, we built a real federated learning system — hospitals can train a shared diagnostic model together without ever sharing a single patient record. Only model weights cross the network. Zero data leaves the hospital.
Market & Opportunity
This is a $5.4 billion market growing at 12% a year, and no existing tool combines AI diagnosis, real-time research, voice output, and wearable integration in one pipeline. Our users are primary care doctors seeing unfamiliar symptom clusters, geneticists wanting a second opinion, and rural clinics with no specialist access.
